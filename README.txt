### Multi Currency Converter features

&#9658; **Multi Position:** You can add Multi Currency Converter to top-left, top-right, bottom-left, bottom-right on
your shop.
&#9658; **Switcher Currency when hovering:** Multi Currency Convert Control appears when hovering on the price.

&#9658; **Switcher Styles:** The App offers up to 6 unique Switcher Control styles.
&#9658; **Converting Everywhere:** Multi Currency Convert not only converts the Product Price, but also converts money

1. Unzip the download package

1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
